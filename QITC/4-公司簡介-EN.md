## About

NiCoreFlow Co., Ltd. is a Taiwan-based AI startup specializing in proactive mental wellness technology. Founded in 2020 by Chi-Chang Yu, the company builds AI-powered solutions that bridge the gap between everyday digital communication and personal mental health awareness.

Our flagship product, **Moltos**, is the first AI companion designed to detect stress from real behavioral data — not self-reported surveys — and proactively intervene before burnout becomes a crisis.

## Product

**Moltos — Proactive Mental Wellness AI**

Moltos integrates with everyday messaging platforms (LINE, Telegram, WhatsApp, Gmail, Slack) to passively monitor communication patterns and compute a real-time Calm Index. When behavioral anomalies indicate rising stress, Moltos proactively reaches out through AI-powered voice calls with real-time emotion analysis.

Core algorithm open-sourced under MIT License: https://github.com/fishtvlvoe/moltos
